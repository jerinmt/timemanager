This is a tool to help people working on laptops and PCs to manage time.

It has multiple clocks to show different time zones.

And other tools like timer, stopwatch, notepad and a scheduler timeline.

    Instructions for timeline

  • Click and drag on the time axis to move the timeline, scroll to zoom the timeline
  • Click and drag an item to change its date, double-click to change its text
  • Double click or ctrl+drag on an empty spot in the timeline to create a new item
  • NB:The timeline may not work properly in mobiles.
